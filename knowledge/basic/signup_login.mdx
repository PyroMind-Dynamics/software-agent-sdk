## Sign up for an account

1. Open the [PyroMind login page](https://console.pyromind.ai/login).
2. Select the sign-up entry and fill in the required information, such as email and password.
3. Complete email verification if prompted.
4. After registration succeeds, sign in to the Console with the same email and password.

<Note>
Screenshots for the sign-up flow will be added later. If the page offers third-party sign-in options, refer to what is shown in the Console.
</Note>

## Sign in to the Console

Go to the [Console login page](https://console.pyromind.ai/), enter your registered email and password, and sign in.
